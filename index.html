Installation

Download than from the link above.

Move the file to one of those two directories and extract it:

-- C:\Users\[YOUR_USERNAME]\AppData\Roaming\Aseprite\data\skins\ --

-- [YOUR_ASEPRITE_INSTALLATION_PATH]\data\skins\ --

Open Aseprite.

Open Edit\Preferences\Theme, choose than from the list.

Click Select then click OK.

Restart Aseprite to make the change happen.
